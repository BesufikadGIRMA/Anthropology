Chapter 1

Introduction to Anthropology

Anthropology means ‘reason about humans’ or ‘the study or science of humankind or humanity.

A major impetus for the growth of Anthropology was the expansion of western colonial powers and their consequent desire to better understand the peoples living under colonial domination.

Scope of Anthropology

The breadth and depth of anthropology is immense ‘Anthropology has humanity as its object of research, but unlike the other human sciences, it tries to grasp its object through its most diverse manifestations
